What is 1A23 Service Bot?

1A23 Sercive Bot is a Telegram chat bot that helps you connect with school online portals easier and more handy. Simply log in with your account and you can access resources right at your chat.

What can it do?

Printable Timetable

Generate a printable timetable with precisely A4 size and beautiful color coding with just a message. You can choose a specific week, in case of changed timetable.

Timetable of the day

Get your timetable of the day with current lesson highglighted to remind you when and where you should go next.

Attendance Checking

Reminds your attendance of the day in every morning, so that you would never have an absent day by faulty biometric station.

Announcements

Delivery to you the most recent announcments from the school. Both portals are supported.

LMS updates

Check every LMS updates for you and get you directly to the file. So that you no longer need to dig into the long list of files.

LMS search

Search the entire LMS courses for files that you need. No need to dig through the course list anymore!

Subscription

Have your favorite function automatically everyday.
